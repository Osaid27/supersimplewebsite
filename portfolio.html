Validictorian App

The valedictorian app provides a simple and easy way to organize information about students in a class to get an overview of the class’s performance as well as how each student is performing. Simply enter each student’s grades for individual assignments, and the app will provide a ranked list of the students, find the class valedictorian, and provide the overall class average.

The Quest For the Dragon

The Quest for the Dragon is a text-based RPG that takes the player through three exciting levels to hunt for and defeat a dragon. Throughout the game, travel through forests, villages, and caves, and try to make the right decisions to survive and defeat the dragon!
